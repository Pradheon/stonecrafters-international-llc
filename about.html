About Us

History

Established in 2016. Since we opened our doors and began designing and fabricating countertops and remodels, we have been locally owned and operated in the beautiful city of Modesto, CA. We truly pride ourselves in our superior craftsmanship of countertops and customer service that is second to none. We strive to be the premier source for custom fabrication of quality solid surface products and natural stone. Our business was founded on the highest standards of excellence; a tradition we extend to each client.

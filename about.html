About Us

Our Mission

Where Your Vision Meets Reality

Stone Crafters is a local small business who mainly specializes in kitchen and bathroom remodeling. With our teams dedication, we do our best to exceed our clients needs in order to make your everyday home become your dream home. Stone Crafters will accommodate to ensure the customers vision comes to life regardless of budget, material needs, and even unique design ideas. You got the vision, we will install it. Our team will work with unparalleled efficiency and professionalism considering that building customers ideal home spaces is our number one goal.

About Stone Crafter's International

Established in 2016.

Since we opened our doors and began designing and fabricating countertops and remodels, we have been locally owned and operated in the beautiful city of Modesto, CA. We truly pride ourselves in our superior craftsmanship of countertops and customer service that is second to none. We strive to be the premier source for custom fabrication of quality solid surface products and natural stone. Our business was founded on the highest standards of excellence; a tradition we extend to each client.

Our Specialties

Over time, our team has grown and improved tremendously. We are able to get our specialty jobs done with ease thanks to our amazing crew. Our specialty jobs being:

  • Stone Counter Tops and Vanity.s (quartz, granite, quartzite, porcelain)
  • Solid Surface Showers (piedrafina, quartz)
  • Tile surfaces, surrounds, and flooring
  • LVT flooring

We personally don't do plumbing, painting, or electrical work but we are in contact with some our favorite local businesses whom we trust to work with. If the customer is willing to work with us, we can help provide these services in order to make your experience remodeling a lot easier.

Disclaimer: While we do work with local people, they have their own schedule and business to run as well. We would recommend to find someone from your end if it seems like there will be any conflict.

As a small business, we hope our customers are able to understand that rescheduling might occur due to late material arrival, sick employee's, and so on. As much as we love to get our jobs done within said time frames, situations out of our control may occur. Having to rush a job and submitting bad work is much worse than lending us some extra time for a quality remodel. We aren't perfect, but we ensure our work is.
